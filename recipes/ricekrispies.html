Rice Krispie Cereal

Description

Snap, crackle and pop til you drop. This delicious breakfast cereal is light, and crispy. Not just for breakfast, it is great as a midnight snack.

Ingredients

  • box of rice krispies
  • milk

Steps

  1. Pour raw rice krispies into a cereal bowl
  2. Pour milk on top. Be careful that you pour enough, if not, it will be dry and gross.
  3. Enjoy!
