Red Lentil Dahl

Description

This delicious soup is healthy, budget friendly, and oh so simple to make! The very first time you make it, you may have to take a trip to the grocery store, but once you have the ingredients, they will last!

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 1 large or 2 small onions
  • gingeroot
  • 2 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 can full fat coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 28 oz can of whole tomatoes
  • dried red lentils
  • lemon juice
  • 2 tsp garam masala
  • sea salt
  • fresh cilantro
  • naan bread

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pot. Add the onions and cook until they are soft and starting to brown. Add the garlic and grated ginger and continue to cook stirring regularly.
  2. Stir in the curry powder and cayenne pepper. Cook for 30 seconds.
  3. Add the water, coconut milk, tomato paste and tomatoes. Stir in the lentils.
  4. Bring to a boil and then let simmer for 40 minutes or until the lentils are soft.
  5. Stir in the remaining butter, lemon juice, garam masala and salt.
  6. Serve over rice with fresh yogurt, cilantro and naan bread.
