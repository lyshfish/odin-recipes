No Name Ramen

Ingredients

  • package of No Name Ramen Noodles
  • water
  • egg

Directions

  1. Boil about 5 cups of water in a pot.
  2. Empty noodles into water and then promptly crack the egg into the pot.
  3. Let simmer for about 4 minutes or until the noodles are cooked.
  4. Empty contents of seasoning packet into the pot.
  5. Add frozen peas or corn for a little extra flavour.
  6. Enjoy!
